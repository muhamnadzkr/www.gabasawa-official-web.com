Welcome to Gabasawa LGA

Gabasawa Local Government Area is one of the administrative areas in Kano State, Nigeria. Our council is dedicated to serving the community with transparency, development, and social services.

About Us

Gabasawa LGA was created to improve administration and provide essential services to its citizens. Our vision is to be a model local government for development, education, and welfare in Kano State.

Our mission includes promoting community growth, infrastructure, education, and healthcare.

Departments

Administration / Secretariat

Handles records, personnel, and general governance of the local government.

Finance / Treasury

Manages budgeting, revenue collection, accounts, and financial planning.

Education

Responsible for primary and secondary schools, literacy programs, and educational initiatives.

Health

Manages local clinics, hospitals, vaccination programs, and health awareness campaigns.

Agriculture

Supports farmers, agricultural programs, and rural development projects.

Works / Public Infrastructure

Handles roads, drainage, public buildings, and community development projects.

Water & Sanitation

Manages water supply, sanitation, and environmental hygiene programs.

Youth & Sports

Coordinates youth programs, sports events, and talent development.

Women Affairs / Social Welfare

Promotes women empowerment, welfare programs, and social services.

Transport / Vehicle Management

Regulates public transport, vehicle registration, and transport services.

Town Planning / Urban Development

Manages land use, town planning, and urban development projects.

Legal / Council Affairs

Provides legal advisory, by-laws, and council-related documentation.

Environmental Health

Monitors environmental quality and ensures public health compliance.

Information & Public Relations

Manages media relations, community communication, and public announcements.

Disaster Management

Coordinates emergency response, disaster preparedness, and safety measures.
